## What works today

Today you can sign up, set your preferences, and generate a meal plan. The constraint solver is real and runs in seconds. The recipe library is sixty Hungarian-aware recipes deep. The fridge tracker is there. Auth, profiles, feedback all work.

That's the chassis. What's coming next is the experience that turns it into Kalmio.

## Calendar-anchored plans and the grooming ritual

The next sprint anchors plans to real calendar dates instead of "Day 1 / Day 2." The planning window will match how often you shop — weekly, biweekly, whatever fits your life.

Before each new plan, we'll walk you through your fridge in a 2–3 minute "grooming" ritual: what's still good, what to throw out, what to use up. The next plan starts from that grounded state, with expiring items prioritized so nothing dies in your fridge unused.

This is the single biggest differentiator we're building. No other meal planner does it.

## The "git diff" replan

When life happens — you skipped lunch, ate out, brought home leftovers — Kalmio recomputes the rest of your week and shows you what would change as a small narrative: *"Wednesday's lasagna moves to Friday. You don't need spinach this week — about 600 Ft saved."*

Accept or decline. No re-doing the plan from scratch. No guilt for breaking it.

## A daily commander dashboard

Today's dashboard is a placeholder. The real one — coming with the next release — shows you today's meals, today's prep tasks (*"start the overnight oats tonight"*), tomorrow's micro-preps, and the rest of the week at a glance.

It's structured as a stack of modules so future pillars (movement, sleep, meditation) plug in without rebuilding it.

## Auto-tracking, no logging

The plan is the food log. Eat what's planned and your day is tracked. Off-plan? One quick entry. We won't ask you to confirm meals you ate from the plan — that's the friction that kills every tracker we've ever tried.

## A Founding Member tier

For the first thousand users who want to back this project early, we're opening a one-time **Founding Member** tier — lifetime access to every current and future premium feature. Details and the live counter will appear on the landing page when it goes live.

The product itself stays free for everyone, forever. Premium is reserved for AI-assisted features that have a real per-use cost — typing "swap the broccoli" and having an agent reason about alternatives, or photographing your off-plan lunch for automatic logging.

## What we're not building

We're not adding workouts, sleep tracking, or meditation right now. Those pillars come later — after we get eating right. We're not adding social features, leaderboards, or streaks-with-shame. The tone is private assistant, not boot camp.
